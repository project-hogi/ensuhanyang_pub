Entrepreneur Camp

Entrepreneur Camp is an on-site program prepared to prospective entrepreneurs (mainly students)’ practice adaptability and instill a challenge spirit by intensively delivering the on-site experiences and practical wisdom of businessmen to them.

  • Participants
    ㆍ100 people including student entrepreneurs, startup clubs, other college students interested in startups, mentors (company CEOs), etc.
  • Major Contents
    ㆍStartup experience learning for prospective CEOs (special lectures by inviting well-known figures, stories of startup experiences from alumni entrepreneurs, etc.)
    ㆍOn-site mentoring by company CEOs (establish startup idea business plans and present on-site by teams, mentors’ one-point lessons, etc.)
    ㆍChallenge program
    ㆍDinner and friendship times

Startup Camp

Startup Camp is an on-site program that provides mentoring and startup experience learning (special lectures and experience stories) by company CEOs for prospective CEOs.

  • Participants
    ㆍ100 people including student entrepreneurs, startup clubs, other college students interested in startups, mentors (company CEOs), etc.
  • Major Contents
    ㆍStartup experience learning for prospective CEOs (special lectures by inviting well-known figures, stories of startup experiences from alumni entrepreneurs, etc.)
    ㆍOn-site mentoring by company CEOs (establish startup idea business plans and present on-site by teams, mentors’ one-point lessons, etc.)
    ㆍChallenge program
    ㆍDinner and friendship times

Techno-Business Administration CEO Camp

Techno-Business Administration CEO Camp is a place of networking where ‘Techno-Business Administration’ students present the results of ‘100,000-won Projects’ and wrap up the classes of one-semester to improve their entrepreneurship and startup mindset.

  • Participants
    ㆍTotal of 500 people including college students and other participants (Techno-Business Administration students, panel of judges, alumni, etc.)
  • Program Details
    Category Details
    Session 1 Team presentation (5~8 teams)
    Intermission
    Session 2 Special lectures on startup success strategies by CEOs
    Conversation with alumni & final evaluation
    Award ceremony
