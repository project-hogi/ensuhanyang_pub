Alumni Startup Sauna

Contribute to a stable business development through cooperation as a common startup space for alumni to foster 1-person alumni entrepreneurs, by providing 1 free seat per person

Support Details
ㆍProvide professional advisory services of legal affairs/taxation/labor/patent, etc. by center partners
ㆍSupport business development such as patent application fee, promotion/marketing cost, etc.
ㆍConnection with the opportunities of startup funds investment through Hanyang Angel Fund and Hanyang Youth Startup Fund
ㆍPreference on taking startup education such as Hanyang Start-up Academy, Smart Creation Site, etc.
ㆍFree participation in entrepreneur nurture programs – Entrepreneur Forum, Mentoring Café, etc.
ㆍCan use 50 kinds of experiment equipment in the common equipment room and library for free
ㆍConnection to university-industry cooperation programs with Hanyang University professors and researchers
