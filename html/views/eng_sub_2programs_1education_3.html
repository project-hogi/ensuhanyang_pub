Hanyang Startup Academy

Hanyang Start-up Academy is a CEO nurturing program that helps improve the success rate of startups and supports the establishment of a stable growth base by providing a practice-focused management capability strengthening education for prospective and beginning entrepreneurs who are preparing for startups.

  • Program Information
    ㆍArea of Startup : No limits
    ㆍEducation Period : 15 weeks, once a week for 3 hours session
    ㆍDetails : Startup foundation, startup introductory, startup application, global startup, financing and investment attraction, performance presentation, alumni networking, etc.
  • Selection Process
    1. 1st Document Screening: Judgment criteria (out of 100 points)
    ㆍPreparation for startup (20 points): Experience in working in similar job types,
    intellectual property rights, networks, etc.
    ㆍAppropriateness and feasibility of business commercialization plan (40 points):
    Concreteness of plan, feasibility of commercialization, etc.
    ㆍExcellence of startup items (40 points): Differentiating factors and ripple effects of
    technology, concreteness, possibility, etc.
    2. 2nd Interview Evaluation: Judgment criteria (out of 100 points)
    ㆍEntrepreneurship (40 points) Entrepreneurship, competence for startups, etc.
    ㆍMarketability (60 points) Market size and advantage over competing products, potential for market advancement, etc.
  • Educational Benefits
    1. Provides business financial aid for excellent students (prototype production cost up to 30 million KRW, etc.)
    2. Provides the benefit of participating in Global Challenge Programs for excellent students (college students)
    (Silicon Valley, U.S., Shanghai, China, etc.)
    3. Frequently provides management support service such as patent, legal affairs, accounting, marketing, channel development, etc.
    4. Connection with the opportunities of startup funds such as Hanyang Angel Club/Hanyang Youth Startup Fund/TIPS Program, etc.
    5. Privileges on moving into BI Center’s student/alumni Startup Sauna and independent spaces
    6. Primary recommendation to programs such as matching with university technology and consulting for difficult technologies, etc.
    7. Free participation in the entrepreneur nurturing programs of Hanyang University such as Hanyang Startup Forum, Hanyang Entrepreneur Forum, Business Hub Day, etc.
    8. Issues certificates with the name of the President of Hanyang University; approves as Hanyang alumni; automatically joins Hanyang Venture Alumni
