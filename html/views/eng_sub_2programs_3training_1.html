Lion Cup Competition

As one of Hanyang alumni success startup projects, Lion Cup Competition is a startup competition for Hanyang Univ. students and alumni to form a team to develop and commercialize a joint startup idea.

  • Purpose
    ㆍFor the successful startups of university students and prospective alumni entrepreneurs/beginning entrepreneurs, this competition aims to create a startup education learning environment of creation, challenge, innovation, and collaboration for Hanyang University students (Junior Lion) and alumni (Senior Lion) to participate together.
    ㆍCreation of alumni-connected startup education environment
    ㆍExpansion of on & off campus startup education
    ㆍIncrease of startup learning effects
  • Participants
    ㆍStudents (Junior Lion): Hanyang University students (including students on leave of absence from school) interested in startups
    ㆍHanyang alumni (Senior Lion): Those admitted as the alumni of Hanyang such as the graduates of Hanyang University (undergraduate and graduate schools) and students who completed Hanyang Start-up Academy and are interested in startups
    ㆍCan participate in teams if Hanyang alumni and students form a team
  • Eligibility
    ㆍAll ideas that can lead to the establishment of startups (no area limits)c ㆍCan apply to this competition even after applying to other competitions
    ㆍEntrepreneurs with more than 2 years of startups since establishment cannot apply with their companies' current main items
    ㆍCan apply with previously award-winning items (but the items must be admitted to be progressive compared to the previously award-winning ones)
  • Competition Schedule
     

Venture Startup Competition

Venture startup competitions is a startup competition in which individuals or teams interested in startups can participate as the undergraduates and graduates of Hanyang University.

  • Participants
    ㆍIndividuals or teams interested in startups can participate as the undergraduates and graduates of Hanyang University (5 or less teammates) ㆍCan apply with previously award-winning items (but the items must be admitted to be progressive compared to the previously award-winning ones; will include ‘progressiveness evaluation document’ in the application form)
  • Eligibility
    ㆍAll ideas that can lead to the establishment of startups (no area limits)
  • Benefits for Award Winners
    ㆍProvides overseas tour benefits for grand prize and excellence award winners (1 person per team)
    ㆍAutomatically selected as startup clubs when receiving the awards
    ㆍSupport Details of Startup Clubs
    ※ Support prototype production cost, patent application fee, consulting fee, etc. depending on the progress of commercialization
    - Provides a common office for 6 months for free
    - Frequent mentoring and support with the experts of each field such as legal affairs, patent, taxation, investment, etc.     ㆍProvides investment opportunities of establishment funds through Hanyang Angel Fund, Hanyang Youth Startup Fund, etc.
    ㆍExtra points when applying to move-in to the Business Incubator Center of Hanyang University Seoul Campus

Campus CEO Startup Competition

Campus CEO startup competition reviews business plans written by startup lecture students for one semester as well as discovers and supports new business models based on creative ideas.

  • Purpose
    ㆍTo review business plans written by startup lecture students for one semester as well as discover and support new business models based on creative ideas
  • Overview
    ㆍPeriod : Once every semester
    ㆍParticipants : 100 people including current and former students of campus CEO startup lecture, chief faculties of curriculum, exclusive mentoring team (company CEOs), etc.
  • Main Programs
    ㆍPresent business ideas discovered and embodied during the semester
    ㆍScreening and award by chief professors and exclusive mentoring team
